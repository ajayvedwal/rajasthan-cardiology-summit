About the Summit

The 3rd Rajasthan Cardiology Summit brings together leading cardiologists, physicians, fellows, and healthcare professionals for a day of academic exchange and collaboration. This summit will feature advanced case presentations, discussions on interventional cardiology, and updates on cardiovascular treatment strategies.

Physician and Interventional cardiologist need to keep up with the newst discoveries and rapid changes in the field, update their knowledge of changing diseae, and learn about effective diagnosis to help thier patients achieve their hightes level of helath. Modernizing their expertise and enchancing their skills can seem daunting because of busy work schedules and lack of time to invest academics.

To bridge the gap, Rajasthan Cardiology Summit, provides a unique platform for sharing knowledge and views, addressing the "Guideline directed" therapy and newer modalities in the pipline. Physician and Inverventional cardiologist from across the country will attent this scientific program, including discussions of various clinical scanarios and interactive discussions.

Eminent Cardiologist and academicians of the country have come together to design the academic content, which in turn, will help you deliver quality care to your patients.

Join us in Jaipur on July 19 & 20, 2025 for an inspiring program designed to enhance your clinical practice, grow your professional network, and strengthen cardiology care across the region.
