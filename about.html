About - Rajasthan Cardiology Summit

About the Summit

The Rajasthan Cardiology Summit is a premier academic event designed to bring together cardiologists, physicians, fellows, and students from across the region to exchange knowledge, share best practices, and present recent advancements in the field of cardiology.

Our Mission

To promote continuous medical education, foster collaboration among cardiac care professionals, and improve patient outcomes through shared learning and innovation.

Who Should Attend?

  • Consultant Cardiologists
  • General Physicians and MD Medicine Experts
  • DM/MD Fellows and Students
  • Cardiac Researchers and Academicians

Organized By

The summit is organized by a dedicated team of senior cardiologists and academic professionals committed to advancing cardiac care in India.
